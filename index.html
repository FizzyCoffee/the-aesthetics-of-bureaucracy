Notice to the Public

This office maintains a blockchain. It has simply declined to use the word, on the grounds that the word is new and the practice is not. A chain of sealed documents, each citing the last so that none may be quietly altered, is the oldest instrument of the State. We have merely automated the clerk.

Everything below is real: real SHA-256, real proof-of-work, real tamper-detection. Edit any filed document's Particulars and watch the Record discover the forgery — and itself — at once.
