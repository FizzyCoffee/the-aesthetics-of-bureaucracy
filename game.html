LETTER OF APPOINTMENT. The Bureau is pleased to inform you that you have been appointed Examiner-Probationary at Desk 7, effective the 2nd of Frugal. The pleasure has been entered into the Record and cannot now be withdrawn, whatever transpires.

Your duty is the oldest one: the public will bring you documents, and you will decide which of them become permanent. What you approve enters the Permanent Record, chained to everything before it, and can never be unmade. You are, in the fullest sense of the phrase, a consensus mechanism with a chair.

Probation lasts seven days. Regulations will be issued as you are judged able to bear them. The Night Audit reads your desk while you sleep. Five citations concludes the appointment.
